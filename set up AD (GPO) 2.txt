Group Policy Object (we already setup the GPO in AD)

1.Create Group Policy Objects (password)
	OPTION1 (GUI):
	open Group Policy management
	click on your domain name then domain then your domain name 
	in your domain name you will see all the OUs
	go to any OU and go to Group Policy Objects
	here you can see all the default polices
	//You can drag any GPO to the OU you want (you can use any of the Group Policy Objects to your OU)
	
	right click on your domain name
	go to Create a GPO in this domain, .....
	give a name for your policy
	click ok
	right click on the created policy
	click edit
	//computer conf. (global conf.) and user conf. (only when the user logged in)
	//Policies (can't be edited by users) and Preferences (can be edited by users)
	
	//here we will do password policy
	go to computer conf. then policies then windows settings 
	go to security settings
	go to account policies
	click Password Policy
	we can see the policies for the password (age, length, comp. , limits)
	
	//we will edit Min password policy
	click on the policy
	(âœ“) Define this policy setting
	edit the input
	click apply
	click ok
	
2.Create Group Policy Objects (drive mapping)
	right click on your domain name
	go to Create a GPO in this domain, .....
	give a name for your policy
	click ok
	right click on the created policy
	click edit
	here we will choose User conf. then Preferences then windows settings
	right click Drive Maps then New then Mapped Drive
	in location input the path (\\servername\folder)
	Drive Letter choose Use first..... then in select input choose the drive letter
	click ok
	
3.Create Group Policy Objects (Desktop Wallpaper Policy)
	right click on your domain name
	go to Create a GPO in this domain, .....
	give a name for your policy
	click ok
	right click on the created policy
	click edit
	here we will choose User conf. then Policies then Administrative Templates: ....
	go to Desktop then click Desktop
	click on Desktop Wallpaper
	click enabled
	you can edit the wallpaper style
	in wallpaper name input the path of the wallpaper
	click apply
	click ok
	
4.Create Group Policy Objects (Restrict Access to Control Panel)
	right click on your domain name
	go to Create a GPO in this domain, .....
	give a name for your policy
	click ok
	right click on the created policy
	click edit
	here we will choose User conf. then Policies then Administrative Templates: ....
	go to Control Panel
	we will choose Prohibit access to control panel ......
	click enabled
	click apply
	click ok
	
5.Create Group Policy Objects (Disable USB Storage)
	right click on your domain name
	go to Create a GPO in this domain, .....
	give a name for your policy
	click ok
	right click on the created policy
	click edit
	here we will choose Computer conf. then Policies then Administrative Templates: ....
	go to System then Removable Storage Access
	we will choose All Removable Storage classes: Deny all access
	click enabled
	click apply
	click ok
	
	
you can create more group policies (creating process are the same)
